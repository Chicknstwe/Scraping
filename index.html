Welcome to Scraping 0.5!

If this is the first time you run this app, it is highly recommend for you to visit the manual section (menu above, "Configuration/Settings") and follow the instructions to install al necessary packages and do all necessary editions to php.ini.

By the way, you should start adding websites, keywords or twitter accounts in the "Settings" section.

At the moment there are X functional features: scraping tool, tweets collector, tweet-to-png converter and excel exporter.

You may find tweet-to-png converter and excel exporter at "Xampp/htdocs/PNG tweets/" and "Xampp/htdocs/Export" respectively.

To view scraping tool and tweets collector results, enter results explorer section. To delete one or more results without removing them from the registers (so the app won't process them again), go to results edition section.
